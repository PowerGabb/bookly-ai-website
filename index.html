Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bookly AI

What types of books are available on Bookly AI?

Bookly AI offers a wide variety of books including textbooks, novels, scientific articles, and children's books. All books are optimized with our AI technology for an enhanced reading experience.

How accurate is the AI narration?

Our AI narration uses advanced deep learning models to produce natural-sounding voices with proper intonation, pacing, and even emotion. It's designed to mimic human reading patterns and can handle complex vocabulary and multiple languages.

Can I use Bookly AI without an internet connection?

Yes! While initial processing requires an internet connection, you can download books and their AI-narrated audio for offline use. This makes Bookly AI perfect for travel, commuting, or areas with limited connectivity.

How does the subscription work?

Bookly AI offers flexible subscription options. Our Pro plan ($9.99/month) gives you unlimited books, premium voices, and full translation support. The Premium plan ($19.99/month) adds advanced study tools and AI-generated study materials. All plans start with a 7-day free trial.
